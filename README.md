This project analyzes a large dataset of over 5,000 cars and BMW global sales from 2010-2025 to derive key performance indicators and insights using SQL.

KPIs:
- Total revenue , average car value , and total units sold.
- Regional Performance: Sales volume and revenue-based model rankings  broken down by region.
- Trends & Segmentation: Annual sales trends (2010-2024) , sales performance by color , and sales volume by transmission type.
- Product Insights: Top 5 and bottom 5 selling models by revenue.

- Power BI Data visualization included
